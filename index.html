Paul Fiterau

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Uppsala University. Previously, I completed my PhD Radboud University Nijmegen before working as a Software Developer in industry for a brief period. My work involves research into techniques for network protocol testing, particularly techniques based on model learning and symbolic execution.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in:

  • model learning algorithms and applications
  • conformance and model-based testing
  • symbolic execution
  • application of formal methods to practical problems
  • network protocol security
